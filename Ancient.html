Roman Empire

   In ancient times, the Roman Empire arose in 509 BC. The Roman Empire was famous and
powerful, stretching from the Iberian Peninsula in the west to the Middle Eastern territory in the east.
For ease of travel, the Roman Road (Via Romana) was built to connect the cities of the empire, all of
which were directed towards Rome. So no matter what part of the Roman Empire you are in You can travel
to all of Rome, the capital city.
