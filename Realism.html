REALISM

   Realism is a realist art that involves an accurate portrayal of the world. Realistic painting often portrays the working class as a form of social commentary. but also extends to new forms of landscapes and portraits.

   Realism often refers to an art movement that began in France in the 1850s. Realism's popularity grew with the emergence of the art of photography, which gave artists a desire to create work that looked "authentic." An attitude contrary to Romanticism This is a genre of art that influenced art and literature in France in the late 18th and early 19th centuries. Excessive emotional expression Truthfulness and precision are key factors for those who call themselves realists.
